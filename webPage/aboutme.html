About me

I am passionate about the Programing industry, as I seek to become a professional in the field. I am also passionate about learning as I believe that the future is only brighter when a person is constantly gaining new information. I value giving back to those less fortunate, through mentoring and tutoring. I strongly believe that education is a never-ending process of intellectual growth. I matriculated in 2015 at the Centre of Science and Technology (COSAT) where we had Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Life Sciences and IT (Information Technology) as major subjects.

After I matriculated I spent 3 years volunteering, mentoring and tutoring students in English, Mathematics and Coding. Throughout that I learnt alot, in the beginning it was just about giving back to the community not knowing that I will learn from the community.
